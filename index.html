Key Features

Everything a rescuer needs in an emergency

Smart First Aid Instructions

Automatically generates condition-specific first aid steps based on patient's medical history. Works for diabetes, heart disease, epilepsy, asthma and more.

OTP Protected Private Info

Sensitive data like insurance, government ID, and address are protected by OTP. Only trusted people with access to your phone can view them.

7 Indian Languages

Emergency page supports English, Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, Kannada, Telugu and Sanskrit — so any rescuer in India can understand the information.

SMS OTP System

Rescuers enter their phone number and get an OTP via SMS to access sensitive info. Emergency contact gets notified automatically.

QR Scan History

Know when and from which device your QR code was scanned. Stay informed about who accessed your emergency profile.

Easy Profile Updates

Update your medical info anytime from your dashboard. New medications, new doctor, new conditions — keep everything current.
